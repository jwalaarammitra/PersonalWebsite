This what I do in my free time!

Meet my dance team, Boiler Bhangra.

I have been dancer on Boiler Bhangra, a nationally competitive team at Purdue University since 2019.

My current board position in Treasurer. From handling my team's financial activities, I have learned budgeting and resourcefulness. As a dancer and performer, I have practiced commitment. Apart from the skills I have gained on this team, I have made strong friendships and understood the importance of syngery.

I absolutely love performing for competitions! This past year, we were fortunate enough to compete virtually despite not being able to travel due to COVID-19. Below is our performance at Buckeye Mela 2021 hosted by Ohio State University in which we placed third. Our performance begins at 1:08:22 and I am in the yellow vardi.

More info: This style of dancing is called bhangra, which originates from Punjab, India and has been said to have began roughly around the late 1800s. It is a folk dance celebrating and honoring harvest.
