Meet my dance team, Boiler Bhangra.

I have been dancer on Boiler Bhangra, a nationally competitive team at Purdue University since 2019. My current board position in Treasurer. From handling my team's financial activities, I have learned budgeting and resourcefulness. As a dancer and performer, I have practiced commitment. Apart from the skills I have gained on this team, I have made strong friendships and understood the importance of syngery. I absolutely love performing for competitions! This past year, we were fortunate enough to compete virtually dispite not being able to travel due to COVID-19.
