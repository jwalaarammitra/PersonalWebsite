UC Santa Cruz Summer Internship Program, 2018

Dynamics Autonomous Navigation Surface Engineering and Robots (DANSER) Lab

Professor Mircea Teodorescu, Jack Baskin School of Engineering


Project Description

Caterpillars travel in swarms, or groups, rolling over one another. This allows them to travel faster and more safely. I worked on computer science and engeering research for publication with Professor Mircea Teodorescu and his 2018 Ph.D. students at UCSC. We created caterpillar robots to mimic the aforementioned caterpillar swarm behavior and to test its qualities. My team and I used Arduino to run motors and Autodesk Fusion 360 for CAD (3D modeling) to create these robots. The image to the right is the final robot design I created with my team.
